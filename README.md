# 🍺 The Dublin Crawl

A retro 8-bit quest through Dublin's most legendary pubs. Navigate treacherous bar stools, dodge aggressive conversations with strangers, and survive the legendary Irish hospitality.

## Features

- **Pixel Art Heroes**: Choose your adventurer. Will you be the "Professional Drinker" or the "Accidental Tourist"?
- **Multiple Pubs**: Each with their own unique charm and ability to blur your vision
- **Zero Consequences**: It's all digital! No actual regrets required
- **Authentic Dublin Experience**: Featuring accurately modeled wooden bar counters and unrealistic amounts of time between pints

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Create your adventurer (name suggestions: anything that makes sense at 10 PM)
3. Navigate through the pub crawl
4. Try not to wake anyone at 3 AM with your gaming

## Technical Stack

- HTML5 & CSS3 (for the ✨vibes✨)
- Vanilla JavaScript (no dependencies, like true Irish stubbornness)
- Canvas (for pixel-perfect pints)
- A healthy amount of `var(--amber-glow)` gradients

## Known Issues

- Game may cause irresistible urge to visit actual Dublin
- May result in Irish accent bleeding into everyday speech
- Side effects include sudden expertise on Irish history
